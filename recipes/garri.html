Garri


Popularly called Garrium-Sulphate because it belongs on the periodic table

Ingredients

  • Garri aka Cassava Flakes
  • Sugar
  • A glass of cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Powdered Milk
  • Groundnuts/Peanuts

It is best to get all the ingredients ready before you start soaking. The reason for Thisis because once you pour the water the garri starts to rise like yeast was added to it. Therefore, everything has to be ready before you start soaking.

Directions

  1. Put some Garri in a bowl, pour in some water way above the level of the Garri
  2. Sieve our the floating particles.
  3. Add more water, some ice cubes, sugar / sweetener, groundnuts and about 3 tablespoons of milk
